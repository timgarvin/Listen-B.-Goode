About This Site

Made by music fans for music fans, Listen B. Goode is a site that provides various Tools and Resources to make the online music experience easier and more convenient for music archivists, hobbyists, radio stations, DJs, music fans, and anyone in general who enjoys music. The name Listen B. Goode comes from and is inspired by the song "Johnny B. Goode" created by the legendary Rock N' Roller Chuck Berry.

About The Webmaster

The sole webmaster for Listen B. Goode is Tim Garvin. Tim Garvin is an Aspiring Web Developer and recent graduate from the University of North Texas with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. He also previously graduated from Texas State Technical College Waco with an Associate of Applied Science degree in Computer Science Technology with a Client/Server specialization. He has always had a huge passion for Web Development, as well as music, and this site is a great combination of the two. He is also a music archivist, music fan, music hobbyist, and a frequent discogs.com contributor. Through his various musical hobbies, Tim Garvin encountered various problems that no one seemed to have a solution for. In response, he created this website full of great Tools and Resources to help other people who are also seeking solutions.
